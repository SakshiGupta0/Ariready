The 3 rules — never break these
01 · Protein first, every meal 🥚
Before rice. Before roti. Before naan. Dal / chicken / egg / paneer first. Always. Mounjaro stops you before you overeat the rest — but only if protein came first.
02 · Zero liquid calories 🚫
Sweet lassi, packaged juice, flavoured milk, sweet cold coffee — gone. Replace with salted chaas, nimbu pani, plain water. This single change unlocks your Mounjaro effect.
03 · Nothing fried at home 🏠
If it's not in your flat, you can't eat it. Stock eggs, Greek curd, roasted chana, bananas, dark chocolate.
