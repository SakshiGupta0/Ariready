3 non-negotiable rules
01 · Protein first, every meal
Before rice. Before roti. Before naan. Dal/chicken/egg/paneer first. Every time. Mounjaro stops you before you overeat the rest.
02 · Zero liquid calories
Sweet lassi, packaged juice, flavoured milk, sweet cold coffee — gone. Replace with salted chaas, nimbu pani, plain water. This single rule unlocks your Mounjaro effect.
03 · Nothing fried at home
If it's not in the house, you can't eat it. No biscuits, namkeen, chips, mithai. Stock eggs, curd, chana, bananas, fruit instead.

Eating out by cuisine
Momos — your acknowledged weakness
Steamed only · with clear soup · max 8 pieces
Steamed momos are completely fine. Fried are double the calories. You get the momos — just always steamed.
North Indian restaurant
Dal/paneer/chicken first · 1 roti · no naan
Eat protein first. Roti not naan. Skip deep-fried starters. Raita is your best friend.
Korean / Japanese — best cuisines for your goals
Order freely from these
Korean: bibimbap, japchae, grilled chicken. Japanese: teriyaki bowl, miso soup, edamame, sushi rolls.
Italian
Chicken arrabbiata · thin crust pizza with chicken
Tomato-based over cream-based always. Thin crust. Eat protein topping first if pizza.
Lebanese / Mexican
Best options for your goals — go for it
Lebanese grilled chicken + hummus + salad. Mexican chicken bowl, skip sour cream. Naturally high protein.
Fast food craving
Grilled over fried · no fries · side salad or corn
McD McChicken Grilled, Subway chicken, KFC Grilled. Grilled is now the default.
